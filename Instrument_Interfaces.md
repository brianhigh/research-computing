* Operating system support

* Quirky behaviour, such as inability to print

Instrument Overload
========================================================

* Too many devices
  * Can your software, hardware, and instruments get along?
* Bus technology limitations
  * More devices = Lower performance
* Resource consumption
  * Bus bandwidth
  * Processing power
  * Memory
